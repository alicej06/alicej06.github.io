# Images

Drop your photos here and they'll automatically appear on the site.

## Hero section
- `alice.jpg` — your portrait photo (used in the polaroid on the home page)
- `hero-bg.jpg` — landscape photo for the postcard (LA skyline, USC campus, anywhere you love)

## Project photos
Add screenshots, demos, or any visual for each project:
- `projects/echo.jpg`
- `projects/alzheimers.jpg`
- `projects/shadela.jpg`
- `projects/meds-ai.jpg`
- `projects/disaster-cv.jpg`
- `projects/robins-cut.jpg`

**Tips:**
- Portraits look best at roughly 1:1.2 (portrait) ratio
- Hero bg looks best at 3:1 (wide landscape)
- Project photos look best at 16:9 or 4:3 (landscape)

All images fall back gracefully to illustrated placeholders if not present.
