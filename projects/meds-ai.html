Meds AI
Full-stack AI platform for explainable medication guidance & interaction reasoning
LLMs RAG FastAPI PostgreSQL Next.js Python Full-Stack

Overview

Meds AI is a full-stack AI platform built to deliver explainable medication guidance to patients and caregivers. Led a 6-member team to design and ship a system that uses large language models and retrieval-augmented generation to reason over drug interactions, dosage guidelines, and patient-specific adherence factors.

The platform prioritizes explainability — every recommendation surfaces the reasoning behind it, citing clinical sources rather than acting as a black box.

Technical Stack

  • RAG pipeline: vector embeddings of clinical drug databases (FDA, DrugBank) with semantic retrieval
  • LLM layer: fine-tuned and prompted for medical reasoning, interaction checking, and adherence coaching
  • Backend: FastAPI with PostgreSQL for user profiles, medication history, and interaction logs
  • Frontend: Next.js interface with conversational UI and inline source citations
  • Personalized adherence prediction model based on user behavior patterns

Key Features

  • Drug interaction detection with natural-language explanations and severity ratings
  • Personalized medication schedules with reminder logic and adherence tracking
  • Explainable AI — every output linked to cited clinical sources
  • Conversational interface for patient Q&A without jargon
